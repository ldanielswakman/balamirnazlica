If your heartbeat is synched to any kind of Electronica, Soaked makes the music you want pulsating through your veins. Here you are in the familiar realm of perfect melodic Synth-Pop, but Soaked easily stands apart from the myriad of others. Lead songwriter and singer Balamir Nazlica makes timeless music, and like his other artwork it has a signature of its own.  Until now, he has managed to release Two albums & an EP.
