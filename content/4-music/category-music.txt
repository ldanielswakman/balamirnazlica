Balamir Nazlica is an Turkish pop music singer-songwriter, keyboardist and composer. His music is best described as observations about love and death, often featuring hidden quotes from his favorite philosopher Nietzche. Balamir worked with different bands and in 2009 formed the band Soaked in Istanbul. Soaked went on to produce 2 albums and a EP, played over a 100 gigs all over and had a rock and roll time. The rest is history. Up and High is his last EP, featuring We Are Lovers which was a song recorded over a decade ago.
