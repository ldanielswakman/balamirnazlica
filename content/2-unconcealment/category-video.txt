Title: Unconcealment

----

Text: Unconcealment is a short film about the calling of Francesco Albano. Filmed by Balamir Nazlica in the studio of Francesco Albano unconcealment is an attempt to see through the eyes of an artist and his mood in the moment of his artistic calling.
